# KANGEN BEAUTE Timeless Radiance Collection Catalog (2026-07-23)

## Overview

KANGEN BEAUTE Timeless Radiance Collection — 3-Step Skincare System catalog.
Two versions: US (Enagic USA, Torrance CA) and Canada (Enagic Canada Corp., Burnaby BC).
Both are 2-page catalogs designed in InDesign (NEW CATALOG-C(2).indd).

## Key Content

### Brand Story
- Premium skincare line inspired by Enagic's heritage of Kangen Water and Ukon
- Designed for balanced, radiant, luxuriously hydrated skin with visibly smoother texture

### Technology Story (3 Cores)
1. **Ukon Curcumin Core** — Japanese turmeric brightens dullness, evens tone, imparts signature glow
2. **Advanced Vesicle Delivery** — Milk-derived lipid complexes + salmon DNA (PDRN) enhance ingredient delivery
3. **Multi-Active Synergy** — 10+ peptides, marine collagen, cica, multi-molecular hyaluronic acid, botanicals

### 3-Step System
| Step | Product | Use |
|------|---------|-----|
| STEP I | First Light Essence | Daytime/Nighttime — refine texture, prep skin |
| STEP II | Vital Rich Cream | Nighttime — intensive nourishment, seal moisture |
| STEP III | Crystal Ampoule Cream | Nighttime + spot routine — deep hydration, targeted care |

### Canada Version Differences
